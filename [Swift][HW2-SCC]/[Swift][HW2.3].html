Cesc Fabregas scored a controversial late penalty to deny West Ham

A controversial late Cesc Fabregas penalty earned Chelsea a 2-2 draw against West Ham at Stamford Bridge. The Hammers were heading for a place in the top four until Fabregas equalised with a penalty a minute from time after Michail Antonio brought Ruben Loftus-Cheek down, but replays showed the contact was outside the area.
